Work done in openFrameworks + Kinect.

It is a work by the artist Lucas Bambozzi exposed in the façade of FIESP building in Paulista avenue in São Paulo, Brazil.

Poems by different authors was exposed in the building's LED façade, where the presentation rhythm and distortion of the words letters was controlled by the flux of people passing on the street. Intercalated with the words, it was exhibited short video frames or just the silhouette of people walking by the building's front.

The work was done in C++ language using openFrameworks library and the people movent was captured using a Kinect camera.
